# 📊 Tableau-Sales & Customer Performance Dashboards

## 📌 Project Overview
This project contains interactive Tableau dashboards designed to help stakeholders such as sales managers, executives, and marketing teams analyze sales performance and customer behavior.

The project consists of **two main dashboards**:
- Sales Performance Dashboard
- Customer Analysis Dashboard

---

## 🚀 Objectives
- Monitor sales and profitability trends.
- Compare current year performance with previous year performance.
- Analyze product category and subcategory performance.
- Identify high-value customers and customer behavior patterns.
- Enable interactive exploration through filters and drill-downs.

---

## 📈 Sales Dashboard – Features

### ✔ KPI Overview
Displays:
- Total Sales  
- Total Profit  
- Total Quantity Sold  

For both **Current Year** and **Previous Year**

---

### ✔ Sales Trends
- Monthly trend analysis for:
  - Sales  
  - Profit  
  - Quantity  
- Comparison between current year and previous year  
- Highlights highest and lowest performing months  

---

### ✔ Product Subcategory Comparison
- Compare sales by product subcategories  
- Compare profitability across subcategories  
- Year-over-year comparison  

---

### ✔ Weekly Trends (Sales & Profit)
- Weekly performance view for current year  
- Shows:
  - Average weekly sales and profit  
  - Highlights weeks above and below average  

---

## 👥 Customer Dashboard – Features

### ✔ KPI Overview
Displays:
- Total Customers  
- Sales per Customer  
- Total Orders  

For both **Current Year** and **Previous Year**

---

### ✔ Customer Trends
- Monthly analysis of:
  - Total customers  
  - Sales per customer  
  - Total orders  
- Highlights highest and lowest performing months  

---

### ✔ Customer Distribution
- Distribution of customers based on the number of orders  
- Helps understand customer loyalty and engagement  

---

### ✔ Top 10 Customers by Profit
Displays:
- Customer Name  
- Rank  
- Number of Orders  
- Total Sales  
- Total Profit  
- Last Order Date  

---

## 🎛 Interactivity & User Controls
